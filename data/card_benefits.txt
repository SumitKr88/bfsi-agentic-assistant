AMEX PLATINUM BENEFITS POLICY (Updated 2025)

1. Travel Insurance: All Platinum cardholders receive trip cancellation insurance up to $10,000 per person, provided the booking was made using the card. This benefit applies to flights and prepaid hotels only. Claims must be filed within 60 days of the trip cancellation date.

2. Lounge Access: Cardholders get complimentary access to the Centurion Lounge network and partner lounges (Delta SkyClub) globally. Guest access is $50 per person after two complimentary guests per year. The maximum number of guests is 4 per visit. Children under 2 are complimentary.

3. Rewards Transfer: Points can be transferred to partner airlines and hotels. There is a 1:1 transfer ratio for most partners, but a federal excise tax offset fee of $0.0006 per point (max $99) applies to all airline transfers. The minimum transfer amount is 1,000 points.

4. Fraud Pattern Library: High-value transactions ($5,000+) followed immediately by a points transfer are flagged for mandatory human review to comply with anti-money laundering regulations. This is the primary trigger for the 'Flagged for Risk' status in the rewards_transfer_api.
